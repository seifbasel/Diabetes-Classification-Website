Diabetes risks

Diabetes is a group of diseases that affect how the body uses blood sugar (glucose). It can lead to excess sugar in the blood, which can cause serious health problems. Some of the risks associated with diabetes include heart disease, stroke, kidney failure, vision loss, and nerve damage .
Early diagnosis of diabetes is important to prevent the worst effects of the disease. Regular check-ups and blood tests with a healthcare provider can help detect diabetes early. diabetes early treatment can potentially avoid the complications that coincide with the subsequent stages of diabetes.
